Baris Manco

Founder of Anatolian Rock

Baris Manco was a very influencial artist with over 200 songs.

His life at a glance

  • 1943: He was born in Istanbul.
  • 1958: He founded his first music band when he was 15.
  • 1958: He took stage for the first time!
  • 1960: He founded his second band named Kafadarlar.
  • 1962: He published his first record album.
  • 1963: He went to Belgium for his graphic design education.
  • 1965: He performed his first concert in France.
  • 1966: He published new record album in Paris.
  • 1969: He founded a new band named Kaygisizlar with Mazhar Alanson and Fuat Guner.
  • 1970: He was married to Marie Claude of Belgium and they divorced in the same year.
  • 1970: He wrote a song called "Daglar Daglar" and this was a turning point for his career.
  • 1971: He served in the army for a short term.
  • 1972: He founded the band that he performed in for the rest of his life, named Kurtaran Ekspres.
  • 1973: He shot his first music video.
  • 1975: He produced and acted in a movie for the first time.
  • 1976: He released and album named "Baris Mancho" and it was distributed to whole Europe.
  • 1978: He married Lale Caglar (Manco).
  • 1979: He released his album "Yeni Bir Gun".
  • 1979: He was awarded "Best Male Artist", "Best Composer", and "Best Band".
  • 1980: He celebrated his 20th anniversary in art by an album named "20. Sanat Yili Disco Manco" translated into English as "Disco Manco's 20th year in Art".
  • 1981: He released his phenomenal album which widely listened across the nation. The album "Sozum Meclisten Disari" includes the songs "Donence", "Gulpembe", "Alla Beni Pulla Beni", and "Ali Yazar Veli Bozar" which are the most listened songs of his. This album was the most selling in 1981.
  • 1981: His first son, Dogukan Manco, was born.
  • 1984: His second son, Batikan Manco, was born.
  • 1985: He released his new album, "24 Ayar".
  • 1986: He releases his last album sold as record and it was named "Degmesin Yagli Boya".
  • 1988: He started his own talk show named "7den 70e" in TRT1 channel. This show was loved by many people in the country.
  • 1992: He released another top-sold album named "Mega Manco"
  • 1995: He released another album named "Musadenizle Cocuklar".
  • 1996: He gave a big concert in Japan and live songs later made into an album.
  • 1998: He broke a record in Turkish Television by airing his 378th episode of his show.
  • 1999: He started to work on his last album; however, he passed away without finalizing it.
